Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

Information We Collect

When you contact Eastbrite by email or through a contact form, we may collect your name, email address, company name, service interest, and any project details you choose to share.

How We Use Information

We use inquiry information to respond to requests, discuss potential projects, provide service proposals, and improve how we present and prioritize our services.

Forms and Delivery Providers

Our website uses FormSubmit to deliver inquiry form submissions to our inbox. When you submit a form, the information you enter is processed by FormSubmit for delivery purposes.

Analytics

We use Plausible Analytics to understand page visits and important site interactions such as contact and pricing clicks. This helps us measure demand and improve the website.

Fonts and Third-Party Requests

Some pages load fonts or assets from third-party providers such as Google Fonts and online font hosting services. Those providers may receive technical request data such as your IP address, browser information, and the page requesting the asset.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We share data only with trusted service providers when required to operate our business, including analytics, form delivery, hosting, and related technical vendors.

Data Retention

We retain inquiry records only as long as reasonably necessary for communication, legal, and operational purposes.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact hello@eastbrite.com.
