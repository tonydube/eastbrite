Our Story

A Reputation Built
One Home at a Time.

Briarcliff Homes was founded on a simple conviction: that the families who choose to build a custom home deserve a builder who is as invested in the outcome as they are.

For more than twenty-five years, we have built homes on lakes, ridgelines, and forest land throughout Northern New England. Each one custom. Each one the product of a genuine partnership between the client and our team.

Our work is sustained almost entirely by referrals — the highest endorsement we know. We intend to earn it with every project we accept.

Founder, Briarcliff Homes
