The Experience

Nestled where the forest meets the lake, The Meridian was built on a single belief: that true luxury is not about abundance — it is about presence. Every detail, from the morning mist over the water to the last ember in the fireplace, is arranged to slow time.

Since 1924, guests have arrived carrying the weight of ordinary life, and left carrying something quieter.
