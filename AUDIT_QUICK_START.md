## 3 LEVERAGE POINTS (Do These First)

### 1. Replace Your Portfolio With Real Case Studies (CRITICAL)

**Current:** Generic browser mockups with no client names, industries, or context
**Problem:** Skeptical buyers see this and assume no real work
**Fix:** Publish 1-3 actual client case studies with:

- Real or anonymized client name
- Before/after metrics + context
- Industry, company size, timeline
- The problem → solution → result
- Client quote/testimonial

**Timeline:** Reach out to 3 past clients this week

---

### 2. Publish Your Pricing (CRITICAL)

**Current:** Zero pricing info anywhere
**Problem:** Visitors can't self-qualify; assume you're expensive or hiding
**Fix:** Add pricing section showing:

- Service packages: "Brand Strategy: $8K-$25K depending on scope"
- What's included per package
- Who each is for (early-stage, established, enterprise)
- Example: "Typical engagement: 6-10 weeks, team of 2-3"

**Timeline:** Done today (2 hours max)

---

### 3. Add Your Team (HIGH)

**Current:** "Team of designers, developers, and strategists" (faceless)
**Problem:** Buyers don't know who they'll work with; can't assess credibility
**Fix:** Add simple section with:

- Founder photo + 2-3 line bio (background, what you specialize in)
- 1-2 key team member names/roles
- "Founder leads every client project" statement

**Timeline:** 2 hours this week
